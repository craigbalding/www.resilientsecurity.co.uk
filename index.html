About

Craig Balding

Founder and Director

Craig Balding is a London based, international cyber security adviser and practitioner. As the founder of Resilient Security, he enjoys designing and creating practical and resilient solutions to a wide range of cybersecurity challenges facing his clients now and in the future.


Prior to starting his own business, he was Managing Director and group Head of Cyber Security Assurance and Innovation across Barclays PLC. He led all aspects of cyber architecture, engineering, assurance, innovation and Red Team. He joined Barclays in April 2014 as Managing Director for Group Cyber Security Risk. His responsibilities included understanding the threat landscape, defining the cyber risk control framework, setting risk appetite, owning policy and minimum standards and assessing global threat management capability.


Before Barclays, he worked at GE for 17 years, most recently as Red Team Director where he led a team of offensive security specialists to deliver a company-wide threat simulation service.
