About

Resilient Security brings extreme credibility to the table with 25 years cybersecurity experience.

Craig Balding is an international cyber security adviser and practitioner. He is the founder and lead consultant for Resilient Security.

Resilient Security can help your organisation quickly identify and manage your cybersecurity risks in a way that aligns with your business goals, risk appetite and constraints. With deep technical expertise, significant experience and board level communication skills Resiient Security provides services to help you get unstuck on risk blocked projects, bring clarity to a complex situation or directly resolve a cybersecurity challenge at pace.

Prior to starting his own business, Craig was Managing Director within Global Information Security at Barclays PLC. He led 120+ cyber experts across Cyber Architecture, Security Engineering, Security Solutions and Security Assurance. He created a dedicated Cyber Innovation team and an experienced Red Team to "stress test" Barclays' cyber capabilities. Before this promotion, he was MD and Head of Cyber Risk, where he defined global cyber policy/minimum standards, acted as SME for cyberfraud and provided check and challenge of the first line of defense. He was chair of the British Banking Association Cyber Forum and influenced Bank of England cyber policy.

Before Barclays, Craig held global security roles at GE for 17 years. In his last role, as Red Team Director, he led a cutting edge offense team delivering major security assessments across GE business units covering finance, banking, healthcare, aviation, research and treasury.
